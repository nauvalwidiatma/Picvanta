## Full description

Picvanta is a fast, private photo editor for turning a subject into a clean sticker or transparent PNG.

Choose a photo, tap the object you want, then refine the selection when needed. Picvanta processes your photo directly on your device, so your image is not uploaded to a server.

Sticker Editor
- Tap to select a subject and remove its background.
- Refine the cutout with add and subtract brushes.
- Pinch to zoom while editing details.
- Add a short caption, choose from expressive fonts, and apply distinct text styles.
- Set sticker border color and width.
- Export a sticker PNG or an original-size transparent PNG.

Remove BG
- Make a transparent PNG cutout without text or sticker styling.
- Refine the selection with the same precise brush tools.
- Export at the original image size.

Made for quick edits and easy sharing. Your exports are saved to Pictures/Picvanta on your device.

Privacy first
Picvanta has no account, ads, analytics, cloud storage, or photo uploads. Your selected photos and edits stay on your device.

Please use photos you own or have permission to edit and share.
